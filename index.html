About me

Hi, I am a health coach and I help women to get unstuck so they can break out of eating patterns that aren’t serving them. I believe that each of us knows what’s best by reconnecting to their own body and spirit. The solutions are already there and I make actions easier for them. I’m a facilitator and supporter of overall well-being and I’m convinced everyone can live their life to the fullest potential. After a free intake, I work with clients every two weeks for 5 months. Every session is on Zoom and 50 minutes long. I can coach in English and Dutch.

Domains of interest:

Cravings, emotional eating, food struggles, binge eating, weight loss Positive habits, stress management, increasing energy

Are you experiencing?

• You don’t feel good in your body/life because of what you eat
• You have a habit of looking for food even when you're not hungry
• You feel ashamed and guilty about what and how much you eat
• No diet ever works
• You don’t have time to eat healthy

Are you looking for answers?

How can I develop enjoyable and sustainable habits around food? How do I break a negative eating cycle in a healthy way (no dieting)?
How do I create a healthy relationship with food?
What do I really need and how can I satisfy this need?
How can I gain confidence and feel empowered in my body?
How can I better nourish myself?
How can I focus on what’s important for me in life?
